About Campus Life Planner

Purpose

Campus Life Planner is a comprehensive web application designed to help university students organize their academic life efficiently. It provides tools for managing assignments, tracking study time, and maintaining academic goals.

Features

  • Statistics Dashboard with progress tracking
  • Advanced regex search and filtering
  • Multi-column sorting capabilities
  • Responsive design for all devices
  • Privacy-focused with local storage
